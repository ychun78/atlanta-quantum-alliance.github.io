About the Alliance

The Atlanta Quantum Alliance is a collaboration between Georgia Tech and Emory University to build a shared community in quantum information, quantum materials, and related areas.

Goals

  • Coordinate joint seminars and colloquia.
  • Connect researchers and students across campuses.
  • Highlight quantum-related research in the Atlanta area.
