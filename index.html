Our Mission

The Atlanta Quantum Alliance connects researchers at Georgia Tech and Emory to advance quantum information science, quantum materials, and related fields.

We coordinate seminars, workshops, and student opportunities to build a vibrant quantum community in Atlanta.

Research Areas

  • Quantum Information & Computation
  • Quantum Materials & Condensed Matter
  • Quantum Sensing & Metrology
  • Interdisciplinary Applications in Chemistry, Biology, and Engineering
